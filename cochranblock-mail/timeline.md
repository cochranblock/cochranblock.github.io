## What It Is

Sovereign SMTP/IMAP server in a single Rust binary. No Postfix, no Dovecot, no cloud relay. Full email stack — send, receive, store — owned hardware.

Part of the CochranBlock zero-cloud architecture: every service runs on owned bare metal.
